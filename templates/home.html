My Journey

I am Moisés Souza Santos, a Brazilian software engineer and full-stack developer passionate about creating high-quality web and mobile solutions. I specialize in building projects with Django, Python, JavaScript, React, HTML, CSS, Tailwind, Bootstrap, and PostgreSQL. My work combines efficient coding with modern UI/UX design, always focused on delivering functional, responsive, and visually appealing experiences.

I value organization, clear communication, and innovative problem-solving. I believe in building trust with clients, creating long-term relationships, and exploring opportunities to grow a software house while maintaining other revenue streams.

When I’m not coding or managing projects, I enjoy creative pursuits like Gaming and engaging with tech communities, always looking for new ways to merge my passion for technology, design, and entrepreneurship.
